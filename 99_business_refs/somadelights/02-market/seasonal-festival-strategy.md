## 2. Diwali Strategy — The Biggest Opportunity

### 2.1 Why Diwali is Critical

- Biggest gifting season in India. Every family gives 5-20 gifts.
- "Gift wellness, not sweets" is a powerful counter-narrative.
- Corporate gifting budgets are Rs 500-2,000 per employee — Soma fits perfectly.
- Gift recipients become potential subscribers — every gift box is a customer acquisition tool.
- Zero ad spend needed. The gift box IS the marketing.

## 3. New Year Resolution Strategy

### 3.1 The 21-Day New Year Wellness Challenge

**Concept:** Capitalize on the "new year, new me" energy. Most resolutions fail by February because there's no structure. Soma provides the structure.

**Package:**
| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Duration | 21 days (January 2-22) |
| Delivery | Daily juice, 6 days/week (18 deliveries total) |
| Booklet | Special "21-Day Challenge" edition with daily tracking |
| WhatsApp | Daily morning message for 21 days (enhanced onboarding) |
| Community | Optional WhatsApp broadcast group of all challenge participants |
| Pricing | Rs 1,499 (vs Rs 2,700 individual, 45% discount) |
| Cost (COGS) | Rs 1,440-1,800 (juice) + Rs 100 (booklet) + Rs 300 (bag) = Rs 1,840-2,200 |

**Wait — the COGS exceeds the price?** Yes. This is a loss-leader. The goal is NOT profit on the challenge. The goal is conversion.

**Conversion target:** 40-50% of challenge participants convert to monthly subscribers by February. A monthly subscriber generates Rs 4,500/month. If 20 people do the challenge and 10 convert, that's Rs 45,000/month recurring revenue from a Rs 7,000 initial loss.

### 3.2 Challenge Timeline

## 5. Ramadan Strategy

### 5.1 Approach: Respectful, Supportive, Not Promotional

Ramadan requires sensitivity. Soma's approach during Ramadan is purely supportive — helping those who fast maintain nutrition and hydration during the non-fasting hours. No promotional language, no discounts themed around Ramadan, no celebration of fasting as a "wellness trend."

### 5.2 Ramadan Iftar Hydration Pack

**Concept:** During Ramadan, the body needs intense hydration and nutrition at iftar (sunset meal). Cold-pressed juice is ideal for breaking fast because it's nutrient-dense, easily digestible, and hydrating.

**Delivery timing adjustment:**
- Regular Soma delivery is 5:30-7:00 AM (before sunrise, during suhoor).
- Ramadan option: evening delivery (5:00-6:00 PM, before iftar) instead of morning.
- Customer chooses: "Would you prefer suhoor delivery (morning) or iftar delivery (evening)?"

**Ramadan SKU adjustment:**
- Hydration-focused: Cucumber Calm (electrolytes, hydration)
- Dates-infused option: blend with dates (traditional iftar food) + milk base (if permitted)
- Avoid: heavy green blends that might be too intense on an empty stomach after fasting all day

### 5.3 Ramadan Messaging

**Week before Ramadan begins:**
```
Ramadan Mubarak to those celebrating! 🌙

During Ramadan, hydration and nutrition during non-fasting
hours are especially important. If you'd like, I can adjust
your Soma delivery:

• Morning (suhoor) delivery: light, nourishing blend to
  start your fast strong
• Evening (iftar) delivery: hydrating, gentle blend to
  break your fast with nutrition

Just let me know your preference. No changes to price or
plan — just timing.

— Sri
```

**What we do NOT do during Ramadan:**
- No "Ramadan sale" or "Ramadan discount"
- No positioning fasting as a wellness trend
- No messaging non-Muslim customers about Ramadan products
- No social media posts using Ramadan for marketing

### 5.4 Ramadan Economics

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Revenue impact | Slight increase — some customers add iftar delivery as a second daily order |
| Cost impact | Minimal — evening delivery route is shorter (fewer Ramadan subscribers) |
| Logistics | Evening delivery in Hyderabad summer heat requires extra cold chain care (see doc 40) |
| Customer acquisition | Referrals from Ramadan customers to their community |

## 8. Ganesh Chaturthi Strategy

### 8.1 Community Visibility at Pandals

Ganesh Chaturthi is a 10-day community celebration in Hyderabad. Pandals (temporary shrines) are set up across neighborhoods, and thousands of people visit them daily.

**Opportunity:** Sampling at pandals is organic and community-oriented. People are in a festive, receptive mood.

**Approach:**
- Partner with 2-3 pandals in Kukatpally/Miyapur area.
- Set up a small sampling station near the pandal (not inside — respectful distance).
- Free samples + booklets to visitors.
- Evening hours (6-9 PM) when foot traffic is highest.

**Pandal Sampling Budget (per pandal, per evening):**

| Item | Cost (INR) |
|------|-----------|
| Juice for sampling (3L) | Rs 750-1,050 |
| Cups (150-200) | Rs 450-600 |
| Booklets (30) | Rs 1,200-1,800 |
| Ice packs + cooler (reusable, amortized) | Rs 100 |
| Volunteer/helper (for 3 hours) | Rs 300-500 |
| **Total per evening** | **Rs 2,800-4,050** |

**Target:** 3 pandals x 3 evenings each = 9 sampling sessions. Total budget: Rs 25,000-36,000. Target acquisition: 20-40 new trial sign-ups.

### 8.2 Ganesh Chaturthi Special

- Modak-inspired blend: coconut milk + jaggery + cardamom (flavors of the traditional modak sweet, but in juice form)

## 10. Seasonal Menu Adjustments

### 10.1 Summer Menu (March-June)

Hyderabad hits 40-45°C. Hydration, cooling, electrolyte replacement become priorities.

**Summer SKU adjustments:**

| Regular SKU | Summer Modification | Why |
|-------------|-------------------|-----|
| Green Vitality | Add more cucumber and mint, reduce ginger | Cooling effect |
| Citrus Morning | Add watermelon | Hydration boost |
| Cucumber Calm | Becomes hero SKU — feature prominently | Peak relevance |
| NEW: Watermelon Cooler | Watermelon + mint + lime + chia seeds | Summer-only hydration SKU |
| NEW: Raw Mango Zing | Raw mango + jaggery + cumin + mint | Hyderabad's beloved "Panakam" in modern form |

**Summer pricing:** No change. Same price, adjusted recipes. Don't extract premium for seasonal need.

**Summer delivery adjustments:**
- Earlier delivery window: 5:00-6:30 AM (before peak heat).
- Extra ice pack in delivery bag during April-June.
- Shelf life reminder: "In this heat, consume within 48 hours, not 72."

### 10.2 Monsoon Menu (July-September)

Monsoon brings humidity, seasonal infections, and lower energy. Immunity becomes the focus.

**Monsoon SKU adjustments:**

| Regular SKU | Monsoon Modification | Why |
|-------------|---------------------|-----|
| Golden Balance | Extra ginger, add tulsi | Immunity boost |
| Amla Charge | Becomes hero SKU | Vitamin C for monsoon immunity |
| NEW: Monsoon Shield | Ginger + turmeric + tulsi + honey + lemon | Immunity powerhouse |
| All SKUs | Slightly warmed option note | "Room temperature is fine during monsoon" |

**Monsoon delivery adjustments:**
- Waterproof bag cover (plastic sleeve over insulated bag).
- If heavy rain: WhatsApp notice: "Rain delay — your Soma might arrive 30 min late today."
- Indoor pop-ups replace outdoor ones (gym lobbies, apartment common areas).

### 10.3 Winter Menu (November-February)

Hyderabad "winter" is mild (15-25°C) but produce quality peaks. Best season for ingredients.

**Winter SKU adjustments:**

| Regular SKU | Winter Modification | Why |
|-------------|-------------------|-----|
| Beet Revival | Extra carrot + pomegranate | Peak season, best flavor |
| Green Vitality | Add seasonal greens (methi, palak varieties) | Peak freshness |
| NEW: Winter Warmer | Carrot + ginger + turmeric + cinnamon + honey | Warm spices for cooler mornings |
| All SKUs | "Try at room temperature" note | Cold mornings — room temp is comfortable |

## 13. Risk Management

### 13.1 Festival Execution Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Over-ordering gift boxes | Start with pre-orders only. Produce 10% buffer, not 50% |
| Under-ordering (demand exceeds supply) | "Sold out" is good marketing. Better to sell out than have excess |
| Quality issue during high-volume production | Don't increase daily production by more than 50%. Spread production over multiple days |
| Delivery logistics on festival days | Festival days are holidays — plan routes ahead, confirm delivery staff availability |
| Weather during pop-ups (monsoon Ganesh Chaturthi) | Always have an indoor backup plan. Cancel outdoor events in heavy rain |
| Cultural sensitivity (Ramadan, religious festivals) | Run all festival messaging past a trusted community member before sending |
