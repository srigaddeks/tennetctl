# 32. Habit Tracking System — From Booklet to Lifestyle

> The product is not juice. The product is a daily wellness habit. The tracking system is how we make that habit visible, measurable, and shareable.

---

## The Habit Loop

### Behavioural Design Framework

Every lasting habit follows a loop. Soma Delights designs every touchpoint around this loop:

```
┌─────────────┐
│     CUE     │ ← Morning delivery arrives at 6:30-7:00 AM
│  (trigger)  │   Bottle at the door. Consistent. Reliable.
└──────┬──────┘
       │
       ▼
┌─────────────┐
│   ROUTINE   │ ← Drink the juice. Note how you feel.
│   (action)  │   Check off your tracker. 30 seconds total.
└──────┬──────┘
       │
       ▼
┌─────────────┐
│   REWARD    │ ← Feel the benefit (energy, digestion, clarity).
│  (payoff)   │   See the streak grow. Get community recognition.
└──────┬──────┘
       │
       ▼
┌─────────────┐
│  CRAVING    │ ← Tomorrow morning, you look forward to it.
│ (anticipation) │   Breaking the streak feels wrong.
└─────────────┘
```

### Why This Works for Soma Delights

| Loop Element | How Soma Delivers It |
|--------------|---------------------|
| **Cue** | The bottle at the door is the trigger. No decision needed — it's already there. Like a milkman, the cue is automatic. |
| **Routine** | Drink + 1-word check-in (in booklet or WhatsApp). Takes 30 seconds. Barrier to action is nearly zero. |
| **Reward** | Immediate: taste, freshness. Short-term: energy, digestion within hours. Long-term: visible streak on tracker, community milestones. |
| **Craving** | After 7 days, the morning feels incomplete without it. After 21 days, it's automatic. After 90 days, it's identity ("I'm a wellness person"). |

## Phase 1: The Booklet (Days 1-21)

### Purpose

The booklet is the customer's first habit tool. It covers the critical 21-day window where a new behaviour either becomes a habit or dies. Every element is designed to reduce friction and increase commitment.

### 21-Day Tracker (Page 3 of Booklet)

**Format:** 7x3 grid, one cell per day. Customer checks off each day they drink their Soma.

```
Week 1:  [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓] [ ]
Week 2:  [✓] [✓] [ ] [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓]
Week 3:  [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓] [✓]
```

**Psychology:**
- Completed cells create visual satisfaction (progress bar effect)
- Empty cells create mild tension ("I don't want to break my streak")
- The grid sitting on a desk or kitchen counter is a constant visual reminder
- Visitors seeing the grid ask about it — organic word of mouth

### Daily 1-Word Check-In (Page 5 of Booklet)

**Format:** 7-row table (Mon-Sun) with space for one word.

```
Mon: tired
Tue: okay
Wed: good
Thu: energetic
Fri: great
Sat: amazing
Sun: strong
```

**Why one word, not more:**
- One word takes 3 seconds. More than one word feels like a chore.
- One word is honest — people don't overthink it.
- A pattern of improving words over a week is visually powerful.
- "Look back at your week. Notice anything?" — the prompt that creates the aha moment.

### Week 1 Reflection Prompt

**Sent via WhatsApp on Day 7:**

> Hi [Name]! It's been a week since you started your Soma routine. 🌿
>
> Take a moment to look back at your booklet tracker:
> - How many days did you check off?
> - Look at your 1-word check-ins — do you see a pattern?
>
> Most people notice a shift around Day 5-7. If you haven't yet, that's normal — consistency compounds.
>
> Keep going! Week 2 is where it gets interesting.

### Week 3 Reflection Prompt

**Sent via WhatsApp on Day 21:**

> 21 days, [Name]! 🎯
>
> You've officially built a habit. Science says so.
>
> Go back to Page 2 of your booklet — the Wellness Check-In you filled in on Day 1.
>
> How do you feel now compared to then?
>
> If you wrote "tired" then and feel "energetic" now — that's not the juice. That's YOU making a consistent choice for 21 days.
>
> Want to keep going? Your next 21-day cycle starts tomorrow. We'll send you a fresh tracker (or use the digital one below).
>
> [Link to digital tracker PDF]

### "Before" Snapshot

**Page 2 of the booklet** captures the customer's starting state:
- How they feel most mornings (checkboxes)
- Their biggest wellness frustration (written)
- One small change they want to make (written)
- Date

**This "before" snapshot becomes the most valuable piece of content in the entire system:**
- It's the baseline for measuring transformation
- It's the seed of their testimonial ("I used to feel tired every morning...")
- It's the emotional proof that makes them recommend Soma to friends
- It's data for Soma to understand what customers value most

## Phase 2: WhatsApp Tracking (Day 22 Onwards)

### Why WhatsApp After the Booklet

The booklet handles the first 21 days — the critical habit formation window. After that, the booklet pages are filled. The customer needs a new system that's:
- Zero effort (no new booklet, no app download)
- Already part of their daily routine (WhatsApp is always open)
- Feels personal (from the founder, not a bot)

### Weekly Check-In (Every Monday Morning)

**Message (sent at 8 AM):**

> Good morning, [Name]! New week, fresh start. 🌿
>
> Quick check-in: How was your wellness routine last week?
>
> Reply with:
> 🟢 Great — I feel a real difference
> 🟡 OK — some good days, some off
> 🔴 Struggling — need help getting back on track
>
> (Just reply with the emoji — takes 1 second!)

**Response handling:**
- 🟢 → "Amazing! Keep the momentum going. This week's tip: [wellness tip]."
- 🟡 → "Totally normal! Consistency beats perfection. Try drinking it at the same time every morning — 7 AM ritual. Tip: [wellness tip]."
- 🔴 → "Thank you for being honest. What's getting in the way? (taste, timing, motivation?) I'll help you adjust." *(Personal follow-up from founder)*

### Monthly Summary (1st of Each Month)

**Message:**

> 🌿 MONTHLY WELLNESS SUMMARY — [Month]
>
> [Name], here's your month in review:
>
> Days on your Soma routine: [X] / [Y]

## Phase 3: Wellness Progress Tracking (At Scale)

### When to Launch

- **Trigger:** 100+ active subscribers AND at least 20 customers past 90 days
- **Why not earlier:** Insufficient data to show meaningful trends. Manual tracking is fine until this point.
- **Estimated timeline:** Month 6-8

### Monthly Wellness Check-In Form

**Format:** Google Form, 2 minutes to complete, sent on 1st of each month.

**Questions:**

| # | Question | Scale | Notes |
|---|----------|-------|-------|
| 1 | How would you rate your **energy levels** this month? | 1-5 (1=very low, 5=high and consistent) | Core metric — most people start Soma for energy |
| 2 | How is your **digestion** this month? | 1-5 (1=frequent issues, 5=excellent) | Second most cited wellness goal |
| 3 | How would you rate your **skin clarity**? | 1-5 (1=dull/breakouts, 5=clear and glowing) | Especially relevant for carrot/beetroot products |
| 4 | How are you **sleeping**? | 1-5 (1=poorly, 5=deep and restful) | Secondary benefit, important for overall wellness |
| 5 | How is your **mood/mental clarity**? | 1-5 (1=foggy/anxious, 5=clear and calm) | Long-term benefit of consistent nutrition |
| 6 | How many days did you have your Soma this month? | Number (0-31) | Consistency metric |
| 7 | What's one thing you noticed this month about your health? | Open text | Qualitative insight — testimonial gold |
| 8 | Would you like to share your progress as a testimonial? | Yes / No | With permission, use for marketing |

### How This Data is Used

| Use Case | How | Privacy |
|----------|-----|---------|
| **Customer retention** | Customer sees measurable improvement → stays subscribed | Data is personal, shown only to customer |
| **Testimonials** | "My energy went from 2.5 to 4.2 in 3 months" (with permission) | Only with explicit opt-in on form |
| **Product development** | If digestion scores are highest → develop more digestion-focused SKUs | Aggregated, anonymized |
| **Community content** | "Average energy improvement across our community: 52%" | Aggregated, no names |
| **Churn prediction** | Dropping consistency + dropping scores → proactive outreach | Internal use only |

## The "After" Moment — Day 90

### Why Day 90 is the Most Important Day

Day 90 is when the customer has enough data and experience to feel the transformation. It's when the "before" snapshot in their booklet becomes meaningful. This is the single most powerful word-of-mouth moment in the customer's lifecycle.

### The 90-Day Reflection

**WhatsApp message (Day 90):**

> [Name], today marks 90 days since you started your Soma routine. 🌿
>
> That's 90 mornings of choosing wellness. 90 bottles of fresh nutrition. 90 check marks on your tracker.
>
> I have a request:
>
> Find your booklet — the one from your first delivery. Turn to Page 2 — your Wellness Check-In.
>
> Read what you wrote on Day 1:
> - How you felt most mornings
> - Your biggest wellness frustration
> - The one change you wanted to make
>
> Now ask yourself: how do I feel today?
>
> If there's a difference — and I believe there is — that's not the juice. That's YOU, showing up every single day.
>
> Would you be willing to share your experience? Even a few words would mean the world to us — and might inspire someone else to start their own journey.
>
> Reply with your thoughts, or send a voice note. I'd love to hear from you. 🙏

### Why This Works

1. **Specificity:** The booklet has their actual handwritten words from Day 1. This isn't abstract — it's tangible.
2. **Attribution to the customer:** "That's not the juice. That's YOU." — empowers the customer, not the product.
3. **Low-pressure sharing request:** "Would you be willing?" not "Please leave a review."
4. **Voice note option:** Speaking is easier than writing. Voice notes feel intimate and genuine.
5. **Timing:** 90 days is enough time for real, noticeable change. Earlier and it feels premature.

### What to Do with the Response

- **Positive response:** Ask permission to share (anonymized or with name). Use in: WhatsApp community, Instagram, booklet testimonial section, pop-up materials.
- **Neutral response:** Thank them, ask what could be better. Adjust product/service accordingly.
- **No response:** Follow up once after 3 days. If still no response, let it be — some people are private.
- **Negative response:** Thank them sincerely. Ask what went wrong. Fix it. Don't ask for a testimonial — ask for feedback.

### The Next Cycle

After Day 90, offer a fresh 90-day cycle:

> Ready for your next 90 days? 🌿
>
> Here's a fresh digital tracker for your next cycle. New goals, new ingredients to explore, same daily commitment.
>
> [Link to digital 90-day tracker PDF]
>
> And here's a new booklet being included with your next delivery — fresh pages, new ingredient spotlights, same beautiful journal.

## Operational Complexity by Phase

### Phase 1: Fully Manual (Month 1-3)

**Time investment:** 30-45 minutes/day

| Task | Frequency | Time | Tool |
|------|-----------|------|------|
| Send weekly check-in messages | Weekly (Monday) | 15 min for 30 customers | WhatsApp Business broadcast |
| Send streak celebrations | As milestones occur | 5 min per milestone | Pre-written templates, copy/paste |
| Send monthly summaries | Monthly (1st) | 30 min for 30 customers | Google Sheet + WhatsApp |
| Read and respond to check-in replies | Daily | 10-15 min | WhatsApp |
| Update streak/consistency tracker | Daily | 5 min | Google Sheet |

**Founder personally sends every message.** This creates genuine connection and deep customer relationships. At 30 customers, this is 100% feasible.

### Phase 2: Template-Based (Month 4-6)

**Time investment:** 15-20 minutes/day

| Improvement | How |
|-------------|-----|
| Pre-written templates for all milestone messages | Google Doc with 15 templates, copy/paste with name |
| WhatsApp broadcast lists for weekly check-ins | One message to broadcast list instead of individual sends |
| Google Sheet formulas for streak calculation | Auto-calculate from delivery log |
| Monthly summary auto-generated from Sheet | Sheet formulas produce summary text, founder copies to WhatsApp |

**Founder still sends messages personally, but prep time is reduced by 50%.**

### Phase 3: Semi-Automated (Month 7-12)

**Time investment:** 5-10 minutes/day

| Improvement | How |
|-------------|-----|
| WhatsApp Business API or Twilio integration | Auto-send weekly check-ins and milestone messages |
| Google Form for monthly wellness check-in | Auto-collect, auto-tabulate |
| Progress visualization template | Auto-generate from form responses |
| Dashboard for streak tracking | Google Sheet or Airtable with customer status view |

**Founder's role shifts from sending messages to reading responses and intervening when customers need help.**

### Phase 4: Fully Automated (Month 12+, 200+ Customers)

**Time investment:** 30 minutes/week (exception handling only)

| Improvement | How |
|-------------|-----|
| Automated streak tracking from delivery data | Order management system feeds streak counter |
| Automated milestone messages via WhatsApp API | Triggered by streak milestones |
| Web-based progress dashboard for customers | somadelights.in/progress |
| Churn prediction alerts | Dropping consistency triggers founder outreach |

## Tracking System Architecture (Simple)

### Month 1-6: Google Sheet

**Sheet tabs:**

| Tab | Columns | Purpose |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Customers | Name, Phone, Address, Start Date, Plan, Referral Code, Tier | Master customer list |
| Deliveries | Date, Customer, SKU, Delivered (Y/N), Notes | Daily delivery log |
| Streaks | Customer, Current Streak, Best Streak, Total Days, Last Delivery Date | Auto-calculated from Deliveries tab |
| Wallet | Customer, Referrals, Tier, Balance, Pending, Last Withdrawal | Referral wallet tracking |
| Wellness Scores | Customer, Month, Energy, Digestion, Skin, Sleep, Mood | From monthly form responses |
| Check-Ins | Customer, Week, Response (Green/Yellow/Red) | Weekly check-in responses |

**Key formulas:**
- Current streak: COUNTIF consecutive delivery days (custom formula)
- Milestone trigger: IF(Current Streak = 7, "Send 7-day message", "")
- Monthly earnings: SUMPRODUCT of referred customers' spend x tier rate

### Month 6+: Airtable or Simple Web App

When Google Sheets becomes unwieldy (150+ customers, multiple tabs), migrate to:
- **Airtable:** No-code database with automations. Can trigger WhatsApp messages via Zapier.
- **Simple web app:** Python/Flask or Node app with a database. Customer-facing wallet and progress pages.
- **Retool:** Internal dashboard for founder to manage customers, streaks, and communications.

## Measuring Habit Tracking Effectiveness

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **21-day completion rate** | 70% of new customers complete 21-day tracker | Ask customers on Day 21 or check booklet at meetups |
| **Weekly check-in response rate** | 40%+ of customers reply to Monday check-in | Count replies / broadcast list size |
| **Monthly form completion rate** | 30%+ of customers fill monthly wellness form | Google Form response count / active customers |
| **90-day retention** | 60%+ of Day 1 customers are still subscribed on Day 90 | Delivery log analysis |
| **Streak length (average)** | 15+ days average current streak | Google Sheet calculation |
| **Word-of-mouth moments** | 2+ testimonials per month (unprompted) | Count inbound messages mentioning sharing |

### Correlation Analysis (After 6 Months)

Test these hypotheses with data:

1. **Customers who complete the 21-day tracker have higher 90-day retention** (expected: 2x)
2. **Customers who respond to weekly check-ins churn less** (expected: 50% lower churn)
3. **Customers who fill the monthly wellness form have higher referral rates** (expected: 2x)
4. **Customers who attend community meetups have the highest LTV** (expected: 3x)

If confirmed, invest more in the tracking system and community. If not, simplify and reduce effort.
