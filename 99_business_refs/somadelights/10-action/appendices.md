## A. Sample Customer Survey (Pre-Launch — 10 Questions)

Use this survey via Google Forms or printed at pop-ups to validate demand before investing in production. Target: 50-100 responses from Kukatpally/Miyapur/Chandanagar residents.

**Survey Title:** "Quick Wellness Check — Help Us Serve You Better"

| # | Question | Type | Options / Notes |
|---|----------|------|-----------------|
| 1 | What area do you live in? | Dropdown | Kukatpally, Miyapur, Chandanagar, KPHB, Madinaguda, Other |
| 2 | How would you describe your current morning routine? | Single choice | (a) Rush out the door (b) Quick breakfast at home (c) Healthy breakfast + exercise (d) Skip breakfast entirely |
| 3 | Do you currently consume any health drinks, juices, or wellness products daily? | Yes / No | If yes, follow-up: "Which ones?" (open text) |
| 4 | How much do you spend per month on health/wellness products (juices, supplements, gym, etc.)? | Single choice | (a) Under Rs 500 (b) Rs 500-1,000 (c) Rs 1,000-2,000 (d) Rs 2,000-5,000 (e) Over Rs 5,000 |
| 5 | If a fresh, cold-pressed juice was delivered to your door every morning by 7 AM — would you try it? | Scale 1-5 | 1 = Definitely not, 5 = Absolutely yes |
| 6 | What price feels fair for a 300ml fresh cold-pressed juice delivered daily? | Single choice | (a) Rs 60-80 (b) Rs 80-100 (c) Rs 100-120 (d) Rs 120-150 (e) Over Rs 150 |
| 7 | What matters most to you in a wellness drink? (Pick top 2) | Multi-select | (a) Taste (b) Health benefits (c) Freshness / no preservatives (d) Convenience (e) Price (f) Variety |
| 8 | Would you prefer a monthly subscription (daily delivery) or order on-demand? | Single choice | (a) Monthly subscription — I want consistency (b) On-demand — I'll order when I want (c) Both options would be nice |
| 9 | What wellness goal matters most to you right now? | Single choice | (a) More energy (b) Better digestion (c) Weight management (d) Clearer skin (e) Immunity (f) General health |
| 10 | Can we WhatsApp you when we launch? (Leave your number — no spam, promise!) | Open text | Phone number field |

**Survey Distribution Plan:**
- Share in apartment WhatsApp groups (ask permission first)
- Print QR code linking to Google Form — post at gyms, pharmacies, grocery stores
- Ask 10 friends to fill and forward to 5 people each
- Target: 50 responses in 1 week, 100 in 2 weeks

**How to Use Results:**
- Q5 score > 3.5 average = strong demand signal
- Q6 tells you acceptable price range — price within the mode
- Q7 tells you what to emphasize in messaging (benefits vs. taste vs. convenience)
- Q9 tells you which products to lead with (energy = greens, digestion = ginger/turmeric, etc.)
- Q10 = your first leads list

## B. Apartment Complex Pitch Script

**Who to approach:** RWA (Resident Welfare Association) president, secretary, or committee member. In gated communities: facility manager.

**When:** Weekday evening (6-7 PM) or weekend morning (9-10 AM) — when management is accessible and relaxed.

**What to bring:** 2-3 sample bottles (labelled), 5 booklets, your FSSAI application receipt (or license), ingredient cards.

---

### The Script

**Step 1 — Introduction (30 seconds)**

> "Good morning/evening! My name is [Name], I'm a resident of [your area/nearby area]. I've started a small wellness venture called Soma Delights — we make fresh, cold-pressed juices and deliver them to homes every morning by 7 AM. We're based right here in Kukatpally."

**Step 2 — What You Offer (45 seconds)**

> "We make our juices fresh every morning — no preservatives, no added sugar, all locally sourced ingredients. Think of it like a milkman service, but for cold-pressed wellness drinks. We currently serve [X] families in the area, and several residents in your complex have shown interest."

> *(If no residents have shown interest yet, say: "...and we'd love to offer your residents a chance to try it.")*

**Step 3 — The Ask (30 seconds)**

> "I'm not asking for anything complicated. I'd love your permission to do one of two things — or both:
>
> 1. **Put up a small flyer** on your community notice board for a week — just so residents know we exist.
> 2. **Set up a free tasting pop-up** one Saturday morning near the gate — 2 hours, clean setup, no mess. Residents can try a sample and learn about our products."

**Step 4 — What's In It For Them (30 seconds)**

> "There's no cost to the RWA. Your residents get access to a healthy, convenient morning option. We've seen other complexes where 10-15 families subscribe — it becomes a community wellness habit. We're also happy to offer a 10% discount for residents of your complex as a thank-you."

**Step 5 — Handle Objections**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We don't allow vendors inside." | "Completely understand. We can set up just outside the main gate, or I can leave flyers at the security desk. Our deliveries are contactless — we leave bottles at the door." |
| "We need committee approval." | "Of course. Could I leave a few samples and booklets for the committee members? I'm happy to come present at your next meeting too." |
| "What about hygiene/safety?" | "Great question. Here's our FSSAI application receipt. Every bottle is sealed, labelled with date and ingredients, and delivered in insulated bags. Happy to show you our kitchen setup anytime." |
| "How is this different from store-bought juice?" | "Store juices are pasteurized and have preservatives — they can sit on shelves for months. Ours is pressed fresh at 4 AM and delivered by 7 AM. The difference in taste and nutrition is immediate — would you like to try one?" |

**Step 6 — Close**

> "Here's a sample for you and a booklet that explains our philosophy. My WhatsApp number is on the back. Could I get your number so I can follow up in a day or two? Thank you for your time!"

**Follow-Up:**
- WhatsApp within 24 hours: "Hi [Name], thank you for your time yesterday! Just following up on the tasting pop-up at [Complex Name]. Would this Saturday 8-10 AM work?"
- If no response in 48 hours: one more message, then move on to next complex.

## C. Gym Partnership Pitch

**Target gyms:** Small-to-medium gyms in Kukatpally, Miyapur, Chandanagar, KPHB. Owner-operated preferred (faster decisions). Avoid large chains initially.

**Who to approach:** Gym owner or head trainer.

**When:** Off-peak hours — weekday 11 AM-1 PM or 3-5 PM.

**What to bring:** 5 sample bottles (post-workout flavours — beetroot, carrot-ginger), 10 booklets, a one-page partnership proposal.

---

### The Script

**Introduction:**

> "Hi! I'm [Name] from Soma Delights — we're a local cold-pressed juice brand based in Kukatpally. I've been a member here for [X months] / I've heard great things about your gym from friends. I wanted to chat about something that could add value for your members."

**The Proposal:**

> "Many of your members are already health-conscious — they work out, watch their diet. But most don't have a consistent post-workout nutrition habit. We'd love to partner with you to help them build that habit."

> "Here's what I'm proposing:"

| What We Provide | What the Gym Gets |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Free samples for 20 members (one-time) | Wellness value-add for members at zero cost |
| 10-20 booklets for reception/changing room | Premium branded material that elevates gym environment |
| Weekly delivery of 5 free bottles for trainers | Trainers become natural product ambassadors |
| Exclusive 10% discount code for gym members | Members feel they're getting a special deal |
| Co-branded ingredient card with gym logo | Gym gets brand association with wellness |

**For Trainers (key influencers):**

> "For trainers who actively recommend us, we offer: 5 free bottles per week + 10% referral commission on any member who subscribes through them. It's not a hard sell — they just need to mention us when members ask about post-workout nutrition."

**Revenue Share (if gym wants financial upside):**

> "If you'd like, we can also do a simple revenue share: for every subscriber who comes through the gym, you earn Rs 50/month for as long as they're subscribed. At 20 subscribers, that's Rs 1,000/month — passive income for a service your members already want."

**Close:**

> "Can I leave these samples for you and the trainers to try? I'll follow up in a couple of days for feedback. If you like what you taste, we can start with a simple 2-week trial — zero commitment."

**One-Page Partnership Proposal (leave behind):**
