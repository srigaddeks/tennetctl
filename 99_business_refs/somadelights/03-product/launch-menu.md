# 07 — Launch Menu

> Soma Delights launch SKUs and bundles. Every product is designed for daily habit formation, not one-time novelty. Hyderabad ingredient costs, home-kitchen production reality.

---

## Design Principles

1. **Habit-first** — every SKU solves a daily wellness need, not a craving
2. **Prep simplicity** — home kitchen with one cold-press juicer, one person, 2-hour morning window
3. **Delivery-safe** — survives 30-60 min in insulated bag without quality loss
4. **Margin-healthy** — minimum 55% gross margin on standalone SKUs
5. **Reorder-driven** — core SKUs should have 70%+ reorder rate within 7 days

## Menu Rotation Strategy

**Weeks 1-4 (Launch):** Fixed menu — all 6 SKUs available, no rotation. Learn which ones sell.

**Month 2:** Introduce 1 limited-edition seasonal SKU per month.
- April-May: Mango Glow (mango + carrot + turmeric) — seasonal, premium at Rs 149
- June-July: Monsoon Immunity (amla + ginger + tulsi + lemon) — seasonal need

**Month 3+:** Drop lowest-selling core SKU, replace with seasonal. Keep top 4 fixed.

**Rule:** Never have more than 6 SKUs active at once. Complexity kills a home kitchen.

---

## What NOT to Launch With

| Product | Why Not |
|---|---|
| Smoothies / thick blends | Need commercial blender, different packaging, shorter shelf life |
| Nut milks | Allergen risk, expensive ingredients, complex prep |
| Probiotic / fermented shots | Need fermentation setup, food safety certification, defer to month 4+ |
| Pulp products | Need drying infrastructure, quality consistency — see doc 08 |
| Meal plans | Need nutritionist partnership, regulatory clarity — defer to 500 customers |
| Detox programs | Medical claims risk, regulatory issues |
