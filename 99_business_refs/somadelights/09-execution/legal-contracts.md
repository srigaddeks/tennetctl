## 2. Refund & Replacement Policy

---

**SOMA DELIGHTS — REFUND AND REPLACEMENT POLICY**

*We stand behind every bottle. If something is wrong, we fix it. Simple.*

**Reporting an Issue**

To report any issue with your delivery, send a WhatsApp message to [NUMBER] with:
- Your name
- Date of delivery
- Description of the issue
- Photo of the product (if applicable)

We will acknowledge your message within 30 minutes during business hours (6:00 AM - 9:00 PM) and resolve the issue within 1 hour.

**Scenario 1: Quality Issue (spoiled, bad taste, off color, unusual smell)**

- **Resolution:** Full replacement delivered on your next scheduled delivery day. If the issue is reported before 10:00 AM, we will attempt same-day replacement.
- **No proof-of-issue required** for first-time reports from established subscribers. For repeated quality complaints (3+ in a month), we may request a photo for quality tracking purposes.
- **If multiple bottles in a delivery are affected:** all affected bottles replaced.

**Scenario 2: Missed Delivery (we failed to deliver on a scheduled day)**

- **Resolution:** Credit added to your account equal to the value of the missed delivery, OR an extra bottle on your next delivery day — your choice.
- **If missed delivery is due to factors outside our control** (city-wide bandh, severe weather, government-imposed curfew): we will notify you and extend your subscription by the number of missed days at no additional cost.

**Scenario 3: Wrong Product Delivered**

- **Resolution:** If reported before 10:00 AM, we will attempt same-day replacement with the correct product. If reported after 10:00 AM, the correct product will be delivered on your next scheduled day, plus a credit for the inconvenience.
- **You may keep the wrong product** — no need to return it.

**Scenario 4: Damaged During Delivery (bottle cracked, leaking, cap loose)**

- **Resolution:** Same as quality issue — full replacement on next delivery or same-day if reported early.
- **Photo requested** to help us identify packaging issues and prevent recurrence.

**Scenario 5: Product Delivered Warm (cold chain failure)**

- **Resolution:** Full replacement on next delivery day. We do not recommend consuming juice that has been above 10 degrees C for extended periods.
- **Photo of product + ambient temperature context** appreciated to help us improve cold chain.

**Scenario 6: Customer Changed Mind / No Longer Wants Product**

- **No refund for delivered products.** Once delivered, the product is yours.
- **Alternative:** pause or cancel your subscription going forward (see Subscription T&C, Section 5).
- **Unused prepaid credit:** can be applied to future orders or gifted to another person.

**What We Do NOT Refund:**

- Products consumed and then complained about (consume first, complain after = no replacement unless a clear quality issue is demonstrated)
- Products not stored properly by the customer (left in sun, not refrigerated)
- Taste preference complaints ("I don't like the taste of beet juice") — this is not a quality issue. We will help you switch to a different SKU instead
- Deliveries where customer was unreachable and product was placed at designated spot as agreed

**Escalation:**

If you are unsatisfied with our resolution, you may request escalation to the founder directly. We commit to resolving all escalated issues within 24 hours.

## 3. Referral Program Terms

---

**SOMA DELIGHTS — REFERRAL PROGRAM TERMS**

*Share the wellness. Earn ongoing rewards.*

**1. Eligibility**

1.1. The referral program is open to all active Soma Delights subscribers ("Referrer"). An active subscriber is someone with a current, non-paused subscription.

1.2. The referred person ("Referee") must be a new customer who has not previously subscribed to Soma Delights and does not reside at the same address as an existing subscriber.

**2. How It Works**

2.1. Share your unique referral code (provided by us) or directly introduce the new customer via WhatsApp.

2.2. When the Referee completes their first paid billing cycle (weekly or monthly), the referral is confirmed.

2.3. **Referrer reward:** 10% of the Referee's subscription value, credited to your Soma Delights wallet every month, for 12 months from the date of the Referee's first payment.

2.4. **Referee reward:** The Referee receives the standard starter kit (free trial week) as part of their onboarding — no additional discount. The starter kit is the referral gift.

**3. Wallet Credits**

3.1. Referral earnings accumulate in your Soma Delights wallet.

3.2. Wallet credit can be used to pay for your own subscription renewals (partial or full).

3.3. Wallet credit is cashable (transferable to your bank/UPI) once the balance reaches Rs 500 or more.

3.4. Wallet credit does not expire as long as your subscription remains active. If you cancel your subscription, unused wallet credit remains available for 90 days, after which it expires.

**4. Limits and Fair Use**

4.1. There is no limit on the number of people you can refer.

4.2. One referral credit per household. If two people from the same household are referred, only the first referral counts.

4.3. Self-referral (referring yourself using a different name, phone number, or address) is not permitted and will result in disqualification from the program and forfeiture of all accumulated credits.

4.4. Fake accounts or manufactured referrals will result in immediate disqualification and may result in subscription termination.

**5. Changes to the Program**

5.1. We reserve the right to modify or discontinue the referral program with 30 days' written notice via WhatsApp.

5.2. Referral credits earned before the modification date will be honored.

---

**SOMA DELIGHTS — FOOD SAFETY AND PRODUCT DISCLAIMER**

*Printed on starter kit booklet and available on request.*

**Product Nature**

Soma Delights products are fresh, cold-pressed, unpasteurized fruit and vegetable juices. They are natural food products, not medical products, dietary supplements, or pharmaceuticals.

**Not a Medical Product**

Our juices are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. The health benefits of fruits and vegetables are well-documented in nutritional science, but our products are food, not medicine.

If you are under medical treatment, on medication, pregnant, nursing, or have any medical condition, please consult your doctor before making significant changes to your diet, including adding cold-pressed juices.

**Allergies and Sensitivities**

Our products may contain or come into contact with the following allergens:
- Celery
- Fruits (apple, orange, lemon, amla)
- Root vegetables
- Honey (in select products)
- Ginger
- Turmeric
- Black pepper

Our production kitchen handles all of the above ingredients. While we clean equipment between batches, we cannot guarantee zero cross-contamination.

**If you have known food allergies, you MUST inform us before starting your subscription.** We will advise you on which products are safe for you and which to avoid.

Individuals with severe allergies (anaphylaxis risk) should consult their allergist before consuming our products.

**Shelf Life and Storage**

- All Soma Delights juices are fresh and unpasteurized
- Shelf life: consume within 24 hours of delivery for best quality. Maximum 48 hours if continuously refrigerated
- Store refrigerated at or below 8 degrees C at all times
- Do not freeze (changes texture and taste)
- Do not leave at room temperature for more than 30 minutes
- If the juice looks, smells, or tastes unusual, do not consume it. Contact us immediately

**Once you accept delivery, proper storage is your responsibility.** We are not liable for product quality issues arising from improper storage after delivery.

**Natural Variation**

Cold-pressed juice is a natural product. Slight variations in color, taste, and consistency between batches are normal and reflect the natural variation in fresh produce. Separation in the bottle is normal — shake gently before consuming.

**Specific Ingredient Notes**

- **Beetroot juice** may cause temporary red/pink coloration of urine or stool (beeturia). This is harmless and normal
- **Ginger shots** are intense by design. If you have acid reflux or gastric sensitivity, start with half a shot and increase gradually
- **Turmeric** may stain clothing and surfaces. Handle with care
- **Unpasteurized juice** is not recommended for individuals with severely compromised immune systems. Consult your doctor

**FSSAI Compliance**

Soma Delights operates under FSSAI Registration/License No. [NUMBER]. We follow food safety standards as prescribed by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India.

Our production practices include:
- Ingredients sourced from established markets and verified suppliers
- All produce washed minimum twice before processing
- Production in a clean, sanitized kitchen environment
- Temperature-controlled storage and delivery (cold chain maintained)
- Batch tracking and production logging for every bottle
- Regular equipment cleaning and maintenance

**Liability Limitation**

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Soma Delights' liability for any claim related to our products is limited to the purchase price of the specific product in question. We are not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental damages.

This disclaimer does not affect your statutory consumer rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
