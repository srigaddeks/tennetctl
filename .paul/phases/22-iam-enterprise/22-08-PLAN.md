## AC-3: Password history check
```gherkin
Given "03_iam"."fct_password_history" (id, user_id, hash argon2id, created_at)

Then service rejects with code=PASSWORD_REUSED
And audit iam.credentials.password_reuse_rejected emitted
When password is unique
Then it's accepted + old hash pushed into history + oldest beyond depth pruned
```
