## Goal
Time-bounded role assignments (`expires_at` on lnk_user_role) with a background sweeper that revokes expired ones + emits audit. Also seed a formal admin-delegation model: `system_admin`, `org_admin`, `workspace_admin` as canonical roles with scope inheritance.

## Purpose
"Grant admin until Friday" is an enterprise must-have (JIT elevation). Named admin roles make the delegation model explicit in seeds, so consumers don't invent their own.
