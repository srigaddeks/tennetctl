<objective>
## Goal

Ship RECIPES + EQUIPMENT verticals end-to-end. User's 2026-04-24 expansion: "play with recipe values" + "raw materials exact for any given day" + "equipment needed for any given day" + "simpler than SAP" BOM model.

## Output

**Schema (migration 006):**
- `fct_recipes` (product_id FK, version INT, status active/draft/archived, effective_from DATE, notes) — one product can have many versioned recipes; only one active per product at a time (partial unique)
- `dtl_recipe_ingredients` (recipe_id FK, raw_material_id FK, quantity NUMERIC, unit_id FK, position INT)
- `dtl_recipe_steps` (recipe_id FK, step_number INT, name, duration_min INT, notes)
- `dim_equipment_categories` (id, code, name) — seeded: juicer, fridge, cutting_board, gas_stove, press, scale, bottler, labeler, freezer, storage
- `fct_equipment` (tenant_id, category_id FK, name, slug, status active/maintenance/retired, properties JSONB, purchase_cost, purchase_date, expected_lifespan_months)
- `lnk_kitchen_equipment` (kitchen_id FK, equipment_id FK, quantity INT, notes) — what a kitchen has
- `lnk_recipe_step_equipment` (step_id FK, equipment_id FK) — what equipment a step needs
- `v_recipes` (joins product + computed active flag)
- `v_recipe_ingredients_costed` (ingredient × raw_material.target_unit_cost × unit_conversion → line_cost; groupable to recipe_cost)
- `v_recipe_cost_summary` (recipe_id, total_cost INR, ingredient_count, computed)
- `v_equipment` (joins dim_equipment_categories for category_name)
- `v_kitchen_equipment` (kitchen × equipment matrix)

**Backend sub-features:**
- `40_recipes` — recipes CRUD + ingredients nested + steps nested + step-equipment links + GET /recipes/{id}/cost rollup endpoint
- `45_equipment` — equipment CRUD + read-only categories + /kitchens/{kid}/equipment nested (attach/detach/update quantity)

**Frontend:**
- /recipes landing, /recipes list, /recipes/new, /recipes/[id] (editable ingredients + steps + LIVE COST display — the "play with values" surface)
- /equipment list + /equipment/new
- Kitchen detail gets "Equipment" section with attach form

**Seed via API:**
- Green Morning recipe v1 with 5 ingredients (200g spinach, 150g cucumber, 100g green apple, 15g ginger, 1 lemon) + 4 steps (wash/chop/press/bottle) — seed 1 recipe to prove BOM cost rollup live
- 6 equipment items: Cold-Press Juicer, Prep Fridge, Cutting Board x2, Gas Stove (for turmeric shot), Digital Scale, Hand Capper
- Link KPHB Kitchen to all 6 equipment items

**MCP walk: final sweep at end of phase 56.**
</objective>
