<objective>
## Goal
Enforce policy-driven session limits: `max_concurrent_per_user` at creation (with configurable eviction policy), `idle_timeout_seconds` via `last_activity_at` column bumped by middleware, `absolute_ttl_seconds` from session creation time. Emit audit events on eviction.

## Purpose
Sessions today have no concurrent-session cap, no idle timeout, and no absolute TTL beyond what Phase 8 hardcoded. Enterprises need these knobs. Policy comes from `AuthPolicy.session(org_id)`.
</objective>
