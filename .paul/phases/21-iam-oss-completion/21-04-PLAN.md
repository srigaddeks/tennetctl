<objective>
## Goal
Introduce a clear distinction between **deactivation** (reversible, preserves all data, revokes sessions, blocks signin) and **soft-delete** (one-way, pseudonymizes PII, preserves audit-actor FKs, reclaims email for reuse after 30 days). Expose both via the existing user PATCH/DELETE endpoints.

## Purpose
Today deactivation and deletion are conflated. Enterprises need to suspend users during investigations without losing history. OSS deployments need true delete for GDPR without corrupting historical audit trail.
</objective>
