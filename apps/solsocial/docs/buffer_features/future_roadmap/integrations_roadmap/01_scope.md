# 01_integrations_roadmap — Scope

This document tracks Buffer's product roadmap for new platform integrations and vertical feature expansions to ensure strategic alignment for competitors.

## In Scope
- **WhatsApp Integration**: High-priority roadmap item for publishing and customer chat (Business API).
- **BlueSky Analytics**: Expanding beyond publishing into full metric tracking for the AT protocol.
- **Threads API Expansion**: Moving from basic text/image scheduling to full analytics and unified inbox support.
- **AI Alt-Text Generation**: Automated accessibility descriptions for images across all platforms using multimodal AI.
- **TikTok Shop Integration**: Linking product catalogs to TikTok videos for shoppable content.

## Out of Scope
- **Legacy Platforms**: (e.g., Flickr, MySpace) are not being maintained or added.
- **Gaming Platforms**: (e.g., Discord, Twitch) are currently not on the primary social roadmap.

## Strategic Observation
- Buffer's focus is on **creator-commerce** (TikTok Shop, Instagram shoppable links) and **emerging social networks** (Threads, BlueSky). A competitor could win by being the first to offer **video-first editing** or **deep multi-level approvals** which are currently absent from their roadmap.
