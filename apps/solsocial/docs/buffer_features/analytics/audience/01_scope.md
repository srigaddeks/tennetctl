# 01_audience — Scope

Audience Insights provides a deep dive into the follower demographics and activity patterns across social channels.

## In Scope
- **Demographic Charts**: Breakdowns by Gender, Age, and Location (City/Country).
- **Activity Heatmaps**: "Best Time to Post" visualizations based on actual follower interaction history.
- **Audience Growth**: Net follow/unfollow tracking over specific intervals.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Time-to-post recommendations must be network-specific (e.g., Tuesday at 9 AM for LinkedIn vs. Sunday at 7 PM for Instagram).
